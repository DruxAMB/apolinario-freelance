Cavalo Na Caixa was one of the first major productions directed by Apolinário and Mariya Nesvyetaylo. It was a co-production with Teatro do Frio, long time partners of Apolinário. It was comprised o of a Performative show, and later an self-edited album.
Apolinário was one of the core members of the project, participating in a very wide creative process from production, concept creation performance, music writing and cenography.

69 Reasons Why I Need More Dic Pics is what happens when Apolinário H and Mariya Nesvyetaylo try to make a performance that fits into the "Performance" type genre of performances it is a visceral ritual of rythmic dance and youtube Y-hole that keeps on giving all the way. It was presented three times between 2017 and 2018.

Translocações da Memória was a collaboration between F and H Apolinário , it culminated in a solo photography exhibition for which Apolinário H did the soundesign.
